# GigaCorp Customer Support FAQ

## Shipping Policies

### Q1: Do you ship internationally?
**Answer:** Yes, GigaCorp ships to over 150 countries worldwide. We have partnered with major logistics providers including DHL, FedEx, and UPS to ensure reliable international delivery.

**Source:** Shipping Policy v2.1, Section 3.1

### Q2: What are the shipping costs to India?
**Answer:** Shipping to India costs $12.99 for standard delivery (7-10 business days) and $29.99 for express delivery (3-5 business days). Orders above $150 qualify for free standard shipping to India.

**Source:** Shipping Policy v2.1, Section 4.2

### Q3: How long does shipping take to the United States?
**Answer:** Standard shipping to the US takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping takes 1-2 business days. Same-day delivery is available in select metro areas for orders placed before 2 PM local time.

**Source:** Shipping Policy v2.1, Section 4.1

### Q4: Do you offer free shipping?
**Answer:** Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $75 within the United States, over $150 for international orders to India, Canada, and the UK, and over $200 for all other countries.

**Source:** Shipping Policy v2.1, Section 5.0

### Q5: Can I track my order?
**Answer:** Absolutely. Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can also track your order in real-time through your GigaCorp account dashboard or our mobile app.

**Source:** Shipping Policy v2.1, Section 6.3

## Return & Refund Policies

### Q6: What is your return policy?
**Answer:** GigaCorp offers a 30-day hassle-free return policy. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and accompanied by the receipt or proof of purchase. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.

**Source:** Return Policy v1.8, Section 2.0

### Q7: How do I initiate a return?
**Answer:** You can initiate a return through your GigaCorp account under "Order History" or by contacting our support team. We provide a prepaid return label for all domestic returns. International customers are responsible for return shipping costs.

**Source:** Return Policy v1.8, Section 3.1

### Q8: Do you offer exchanges?
**Answer:** Yes, we offer free exchanges for defective or damaged items. For size or color exchanges, standard shipping rates apply. Exchanges must be requested within 14 days of delivery.

**Source:** Return Policy v1.8, Section 4.0

### Q9: What items are non-returnable?
**Answer:** Customized or personalized products, perishable goods, digital downloads, and gift cards cannot be returned. Software licenses are refundable only if the activation code has not been used.

**Source:** Return Policy v1.8, Section 5.2

## Service Tiers & Plans

### Q13: What service tiers does GigaCorp offer?
**Answer:** GigaCorp offers three service tiers:
- **Basic (Free):** Standard shipping, email support, 30-day returns
- **Pro ($9.99/month):** Free express shipping, priority support, 60-day returns, exclusive deals
- **Enterprise (Custom pricing):** Dedicated account manager, API access, custom integrations, SLA guarantees, 24/7 phone support

**Source:** Service Tiers v2.5, Section 1.0

### Q14: What are the benefits of the Pro plan?
**Answer:** Pro plan subscribers enjoy:
- Free express shipping on all orders
- Priority customer support (faster response times)
- Extended 60-day return window
- Early access to new products and sales
- 10% discount on all purchases
- Free gift wrapping

**Source:** Service Tiers v2.5, Section 2.1

### Q15: How do I upgrade my service tier?
**Answer:** You can upgrade your service tier anytime from your account settings under "Subscription & Billing." Upgrades take effect immediately. If you upgrade from Basic to Pro mid-month, you will be charged a prorated amount.

**Source:** Service Tiers v2.5, Section 3.2

### Q16: Can I cancel my Pro subscription?
**Answer:** Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time from your account settings. You will continue to have access to Pro benefits until the end of your current billing period. We do not offer refunds for partial months.

**Source:** Service Tiers v2.5, Section 3.5

## Account & Orders

### Q17: How do I create a GigaCorp account?
**Answer:** You can create an account by clicking "Sign Up" on our website or mobile app. You will need to provide your email address, create a password, and verify your email. You can also sign up using your Google, Apple, or Microsoft account.

**Source:** Account Management v1.2, Section 1.1

### Q18: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
**Answer:** Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your registered email address. We will send you a password reset link that is valid for 24 hours. For security reasons, we cannot reset passwords over the phone.

**Source:** Account Management v1.2, Section 2.3

### Q19: How do I check my order status?
**Answer:** Log into your GigaCorp account and navigate to "Order History." Click on any order to see real-time status updates including: Order Placed, Processing, Shipped, In Transit, Out for Delivery, and Delivered.

**Source:** Account Management v1.2, Section 3.1

### Q20: Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?
**Answer:** Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement through your account dashboard. After 1 hour, the order enters our fulfillment center and cannot be changed. Please contact support for urgent requests.

**Source:** Account Management v1.2, Section 3.4

## Payments & Billing

### Q21: What payment methods do you accept?
**Answer:** We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and GigaCorp Store Credit. For Enterprise customers, we also support wire transfers and purchase orders.

**Source:** Payments v2.0, Section 1.0

### Q22: Is my payment information secure?
**Answer:** Absolutely. GigaCorp is PCI DSS Level 1 compliant. All payment data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption. We never store your full credit card number on our servers. All transactions are processed through Stripe and PayPal's secure infrastructure.

**Source:** Payments v2.0, Section 2.1

### Q23: When will I be charged for my order?
**Answer:** Your payment method is authorized at the time of purchase, but you are only charged when your order ships. For pre-order items, you are charged when the item is released and ships.

**Source:** Payments v2.0, Section 3.0

### Q24: Do you offer installment payments?
**Answer:** Yes, we offer Buy Now Pay Later options through Klarna and Affirm. You can split your purchase into 4 interest-free payments or choose longer-term financing options. This is available for orders between $50 and $2,000.

**Source:** Payments v2.0, Section 4.2
